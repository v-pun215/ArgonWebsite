If you get a warning that the app cannot be opened, click 'Run Anyways'. You may need to click 'More info' to see this option. This warning is entirely harmless and only shows because the app is not signed. Signing it would cost me a lot of money.

Currently, macOS is not supported. I have been working on bringing support for macOS. Thank you for your patience. Please check back later for updates on macOS support. I am working hard to bring you the best experience possible.

Currently, Linux is not supported. I am actively working on bringing Linux support to Argon. The development is in its final stages, and I am conducting thorough testing to ensure a smooth and stable experience for Linux users.
